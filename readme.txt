== Description ==
Pretio is the first and only rewards & loyalty plugin that offers publishers the ability to increase and monetize user activity. 
Immediately after installation, your site has its own rewards section in the form of a side tab or a bottom toolbar. 
Your rewards catalog is automatically filled by Pretio with valuable, real world rewards such as gift cards and exclusive coupons from 
top brands. And each time your users redeem points for rewards, you get paid – as much as $4 per redemption!
  
Once Pretio is activated on your site, users automatically earn points – no sign up required – for valuable actions that you decide. 
Whether it’s social sharing, visiting, commenting, or liking, Pretio incentivizes your users to visit more frequently, engage more deeply 
and spread the word to their friends.
  
**Key features for your readers:**  

* Users redeem their points for quality, sponsored rewards (gift card & high value coupons).
* Users start earning points automatically – only sign up to receive a reward.
* Users earn points for daily visits.
* Users earn points for commenting.
* Users earn points Facebook likes.
* Users earn points for Tweets.

**Key features for you:**

* Generate revenue from the rewards your users claim.
* Increase site engagement.
* Increase new and repeat traffic.
* Customize the rewards you offer.
* Keep track of actions taken by each reader and learn more about them.

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

For an automatic installation through WordPress:

1.	Go to the 'Add New' plugins screen in your WordPress admin area
2.	Search for 'Pretio'
3.	Click 'Install Now' and activate the plugin
4.	Register for a free account and Publisher Key here: [www.PretioInteractive.com](http://www.PretioInteractive.com "www.PretioInteractive.com").

For a manual installation via FTP:

1.	Upload the Pretio folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2.	Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in your WordPress admin area
3.	Register for a free account and Publisher Key here: [www.PretioInteractive.com](http://www.PretioInteractive.com "www.PretioInteractive.com").

To upload the plugin through WordPress, instead of FTP:

1.	Upload the downloaded zip file on the 'Add New' plugins screen (see the 'Upload' tab) in your WordPress admin area and activate.
2.	Register for a free account and Publisher Key here: [www.PretioInteractive.com](http://www.PretioInteractive.com "www.PretioInteractive.com").



== Frequently Asked Questions ==

You can find the full FAQ section and get support [here](http://pretiointeractive.com/point-earners-faq "www.PretioInteractive.com")

Q: Is Pretio really free?
A: Yes, you can reward site visitors for daily visits, Facebook likes and Twitter follows and much more.

Q: How do I make money for my blog?
A: Every time your users redeem points for rewards, you get paid – usually about 10% of the value of the reward. The more users redeem, the more you make!

Q: Can I generate my own actions to reward for?
A: Absolutely… If the action can be tracked, it can be rewarded! Reach out and we’ll help you add the new actions.
